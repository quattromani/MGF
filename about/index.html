Michael Goffredo was fun loving, creative and very loyal. He was born on August 5, 1986 in Cherry Hill NJ and left this life on November 26, 2009 in Santa Ana, CA. Michael lived in Libertyville, IL for most of his life. He attended the Culinary Institute of America in Denver, Colorado where Michael achieved “Dean’s Honor Roll” status.

He will always be remembered as a gentle soul with a wonderful smile, kind and generous to everyone.

The Michael Goffredo Foundation was established for drug abuse education and prevention. Please join our family in helping other children and families fighting addition. It is a life long fight that many times claims the lives of the people with the disease and devastates families.

Michael left this life on Thanksgiving and the Michael Goffredo Foundation will also feed the hungry during the Thanksgiving holiday in his memory.
